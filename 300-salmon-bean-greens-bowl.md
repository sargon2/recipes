# 300 Calorie Salmon Bean Greens Bowl

A savory, high-protein, higher-fiber snack for when a sweet dairy bowl is not satisfying enough.

## Ingredients

- 1 5 oz can salmon, drained
- 80 g black beans, drained and rinsed
- 50 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- Mustard, to taste
- White vinegar, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Optional: sauerkraut on the side

## Instructions

1. Drain the salmon.
2. Drain and rinse the black beans.
3. Add the greens to a bowl.
4. Add the salmon and black beans.
5. Season with mustard, white vinegar, and black pepper.
6. Add sauerkraut on the side if desired.

## Notes

- This is more meal-like than the yogurt or cottage cheese snack bowls.
- Do not add mayo by default; it pushes the snack above the 300-calorie target quickly.
- If using a higher-calorie canned salmon, reduce the black beans slightly.
- This works best as an afternoon snack or as a more substantial evening snack when hunger is stronger.
