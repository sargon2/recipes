# 700 Calorie Shrimp Rice Broccoli Avocado Bowl

A single-serving freezer-and-pantry dinner built around shrimp, brown rice, broccoli, and half an avocado.

## Ingredients

- 75 g dry brown rice, cooked, or about 225 g cooked brown rice
- 225 g frozen shrimp, thawed
- 250 g frozen broccoli
- 1/2 medium avocado
- 50 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- Mustard, to taste
- White vinegar, to taste
- Garlic powder, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Iodized salt, to taste
- Optional: hot sauce or red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Cook the brown rice, or reheat cooked brown rice if already prepared.
2. Thaw the shrimp if needed.
3. Steam or microwave the broccoli until hot.
4. Cook the shrimp in a pan or by steaming until just cooked through.
5. Add the rice, broccoli, greens, and shrimp to a bowl.
6. Add 1/2 avocado on top.
7. Season with mustard, white vinegar, garlic powder, black pepper, iodized salt, and hot sauce or red pepper flakes if desired.

## Notes

- This is intended to use 1/2 avocado rather than a weighed amount.
- Do not add oil by default; the avocado already provides the fat for the meal.
- If using cooked rice from a batch, use about 225 g cooked rice as the target portion.
- If the meal comes out short of 700 calories because the avocado is small, add a piece of fresh fruit rather than adding oil.
