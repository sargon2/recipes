# 800 Calorie Avocado Toast with Cottage Cheese

A high-protein brunch built around avocado egg toast, cottage cheese, and berries.

## Ingredients

### Avocado egg toast

- 2 slices whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories per slice
- 1/2 medium avocado
- 2 boiled eggs
- Everything bagel seasoning, to taste

### Side bowl

- 300 g 2% cottage cheese
- 200 g frozen berries

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread.
2. Mash the avocado across both slices of toast.
3. Slice the boiled eggs and place them on the avocado toast, or eat them on the side.
4. Top the toast with everything bagel seasoning.
5. Serve with a bowl of cottage cheese and frozen berries.

## Notes

- Do not add extra salt by default; everything bagel seasoning and cottage cheese are already salty.
- Use 1/2 avocado for the standard version. A whole avocado should be treated as a higher-calorie variant, not the default.
- The cottage cheese is what makes this brunch high-protein. Do not remove it unless replacing it with another high-protein side.
