# 700 Calorie Oat Bowl with Almonds

A high-protein, high-fiber brunch built around overnight oats, Greek yogurt, berries, almonds, and boiled eggs.

## Ingredients

### Overnight oats

- 45 g rolled oats
- 18 g chia seeds
- 190 g water
- Pinch of iodized salt

### Morning additions

- 150 g plain 2% Greek yogurt
- 125 g frozen berries
- 20 g almonds

### Side

- 2 boiled eggs

## Instructions

1. The night before, combine the rolled oats, chia seeds, water, and iodized salt in a container.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
3. In the morning, stir the oats. Add the Greek yogurt and frozen berries.
4. Top with the almonds, or eat them on the side if you want them to stay crunchy.
5. Eat with 2 boiled eggs on the side.

## Notes

- Compared with the 800-calorie version, this reduces rolled oats from 60 g to 45 g, Greek yogurt from 175 g to 150 g, and frozen berries from 150 g to 125 g.
- It keeps the full 20 g almond portion and both eggs, which preserves more protein, fat, and satiety than removing an egg.
- If you forget to make the oats the night before, make the jar in the morning and let it sit on the counter for 20 minutes instead.
- Use 180 g water for a denser, spoonable texture.
- Use 200 g water for a looser texture closer to a softer overnight-oats bowl.
- Add the yogurt fresh in the morning rather than soaking it overnight.
- Do not add toast or extra fruit by default; those should be swaps, not additions, if keeping this near 700 calories.
