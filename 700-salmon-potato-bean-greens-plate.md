# 700 Calorie Salmon Potato Bean Greens Plate

A single-serving pantry-and-staples dinner built around canned salmon, a russet potato, black beans, greens, and half an avocado.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium russet potato
- 1 5 oz can salmon, drained
- 130 g black beans, drained and rinsed
- 100 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- 1/2 medium avocado
- Mustard, to taste
- White vinegar, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Iodized salt, to taste
- Optional: sauerkraut on the side
- Optional: hot sauce or red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Microwave or bake the russet potato until tender.
2. Drain the canned salmon.
3. Drain and rinse the black beans.
4. Warm the black beans and greens together, or serve the greens raw if preferred.
5. Split the potato and season it with iodized salt and black pepper.
6. Plate the potato with the salmon, beans, greens, and 1/2 avocado.
7. Add mustard, white vinegar, hot sauce, or red pepper flakes as desired.
8. Add sauerkraut on the side if desired.

## Notes

- This is a no-cook-or-low-cook dinner if the potato is microwaved.
- Do not add mayo by default; it pushes the meal above the 700-calorie target quickly.
- The potato and beans are doing most of the fiber work. The salmon and beans are doing most of the protein work.
- If the potato is unusually large, reduce the black beans slightly.
- If the avocado is unusually small, add a piece of fresh fruit if the meal does not feel complete.
