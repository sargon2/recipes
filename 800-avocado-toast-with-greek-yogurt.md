# 800 Calorie Avocado Toast with Greek Yogurt

A high-protein brunch built around avocado egg toast, a Greek yogurt and berry bowl, and a measured portion of almonds.

## Ingredients

### Avocado egg toast

- 2 slices whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories per slice
- 1/2 medium avocado
- 2 boiled eggs
- Everything bagel seasoning, to taste

### Side bowl

- 250 g plain 2% Greek yogurt
- 200 g frozen berries

### Almonds

- 12 g almonds, weighed before serving

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread.
2. Mash the avocado across both slices of toast.
3. Slice the boiled eggs and place them on the avocado toast, or eat them on the side.
4. Top the toast with everything bagel seasoning.
5. Serve with a bowl of Greek yogurt and frozen berries.
6. Eat the measured almonds alongside the meal, or chop them and add them to the yogurt bowl.

## Notes

- Do not add extra salt by default; everything bagel seasoning is already salty.
- Use 1/2 avocado for the standard version. A whole avocado should be treated as a higher-calorie variant, not the default.
- Use plain 2% Greek yogurt. Nonfat or whole-milk yogurt will change the calorie total.
- The calorie estimate assumes Greek yogurt near 70 calories per 100 g and bread near 90 calories per slice.
- Weigh the almonds rather than estimating the portion. Store the remaining almonds out of reach or in the K-safe after portioning if needed.
