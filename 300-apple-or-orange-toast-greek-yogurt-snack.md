# 300 Calorie Apple or Orange Toast and Greek Yogurt Snack

A simple afternoon snack combining whole fruit and toast with enough Greek yogurt to make the snack more filling and protein-balanced.

## Ingredients

Choose one version:

### Apple Version

- 1 medium apple, about 95 calories
- 1 slice whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories
- 153 g plain 2% Greek yogurt, about 115 calories

### Orange Version

- 1 medium orange, about 62 calories
- 1 slice whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories
- 197 g plain 2% Greek yogurt, about 148 calories

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread.
2. Wash the apple, or peel the orange.
3. Weigh the Greek yogurt for the selected version.
4. Serve the fruit, toast, and Greek yogurt together. Eat the yogurt separately or use it as a dip for the fruit.

## Notes

- One toast slice works better than two here. Two slices use 180 calories and leave too little room for a substantial protein serving, especially with an apple.
- The listed yogurt amounts assume 75 calories per 100 g. Adjust the amount if the nutrition label differs: subtract the fruit and toast calories from 300, then portion enough yogurt to supply the remaining calories.
- A larger or smaller piece of fruit can move the total noticeably. For tighter calorie accuracy, use the fruit's labeled or weighed calorie estimate rather than relying only on the count.
- Cinnamon can be added to the yogurt or apple without materially changing the calories.
