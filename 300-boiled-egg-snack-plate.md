# 300 Calorie Boiled Egg Snack Plate

A simple savory snack plate using boiled eggs, toast, carrots, mustard, and sauerkraut.

## Ingredients

- 2 boiled eggs
- 1 slice whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories
- 150 g baby carrots
- Mustard, to taste
- Sauerkraut, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Optional: everything bagel seasoning

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread.
2. Peel or open the boiled eggs.
3. Serve the eggs with the toast, carrots, mustard, and sauerkraut.
4. Add black pepper or everything bagel seasoning if desired.

## Notes

- This is a good snack when opening a can of chicken or salmon feels like too much effort.
- Do not add mayo by default; it pushes the snack above the 300-calorie target quickly.
- Everything bagel seasoning is good here, but avoid adding extra salt if using it.
