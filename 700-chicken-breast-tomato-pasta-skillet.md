# 700 Calorie Chicken Breast Tomato Pasta Skillet

A single-serving chicken pasta dinner using one chicken breast, whole wheat pasta, tomatoes, and greens.

## Ingredients

- 1 skinless chicken breast
- 75 g dry whole wheat pasta
- 200 g crushed tomatoes
- 100 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or garlic powder to taste
- Italian seasoning, to taste
- Smoked paprika, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Iodized salt, to taste
- Optional: red pepper flakes
- Optional: white vinegar, to finish
- Optional: light spray avocado oil

## Instructions

1. Cook the pasta until tender.
2. Season the chicken breast with iodized salt, black pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and Italian seasoning.
3. Cook the chicken in a skillet with a light spray of avocado oil if needed, or use pre-roasted chicken.
4. Cook until the thickest part reaches 165°F on an instant-read thermometer.
5. Rest the chicken for 5 minutes, then slice.
6. Warm the crushed tomatoes with garlic, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes if using.
7. Stir in the greens until wilted.
8. Combine the cooked pasta with the tomato and greens sauce.
9. Serve with the sliced chicken breast on top.
10. Add a splash of white vinegar if the sauce tastes flat.

## Notes

- This is the fresh-chicken version of a pasta dinner. Use the pantry chicken pasta recipe when you do not want to cook raw chicken.
- The chicken breast size drives the calorie range. A very large breast may push the meal above 700 calories.
- If the chicken breast is unusually small, add a piece of fresh fruit or increase the pasta to 85 g dry.
