# 300 Calorie Miso Tofu Soup Snack

A warm, savory snack for hunger relief or food-noise pressure release.

## Ingredients

- 18 g dashi-flavored miso powder or paste
- 100 g medium tofu, cubed
- 2 g dried wakame
- Scallions, to taste
- 400 g water, or 350-450 g to preferred strength
- 1 boiled egg
- 100 g baby carrots on the side

## Instructions

1. Heat the water until hot but not aggressively boiling.
2. Stir in the miso until dissolved.
3. Add the tofu and wakame.
4. Heat gently until the tofu is warm and the wakame has expanded.
5. Add scallions.
6. Eat with 1 boiled egg and 100 g baby carrots on the side.

## Notes

- The soup itself is intentionally light; the egg and carrots bring the snack closer to 300 calories.
- 18 g miso is the standard amount. 13 g may taste weak.
- 100 g tofu is the standard amount. 130 g can feel like too much tofu for this snack.
- This is especially useful when you want something warm and salty rather than sweet.
