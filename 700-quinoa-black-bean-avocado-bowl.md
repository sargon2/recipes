# 700 Calorie Quinoa Black Bean Avocado Bowl

A high-fiber single-serving dinner built around quinoa, black beans, greens, yogurt sauce, and half an avocado.

## Ingredients

- 70 g dry quinoa, cooked
- 130 g black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 medium avocado
- 150 g plain 2% Greek yogurt
- 100 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- 150 g diced tomatoes, drained if very watery
- 1/4 large yellow onion, diced, optional
- Garlic powder, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Iodized salt, to taste
- White vinegar, to taste
- Optional: hot sauce or red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Cook the quinoa.
2. Warm the black beans, tomatoes, onion if using, and greens until hot and the greens are wilted.
3. Stir the Greek yogurt with garlic powder, black pepper, iodized salt, and a splash of white vinegar to make a quick sauce.
4. Add the cooked quinoa to a bowl.
5. Top with the bean and greens mixture.
6. Add 1/2 avocado.
7. Add the yogurt sauce and hot sauce or red pepper flakes if desired.

## Notes

- This is lower-protein than the meat-based dinners but much higher-fiber.
- Use 1/2 avocado rather than weighing the avocado.
- If the avocado is unusually large, use a little less yogurt or beans.
- If the bowl tastes flat, add more vinegar and salt before adding more calories.
