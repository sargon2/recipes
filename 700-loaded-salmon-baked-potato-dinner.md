# 700 Calorie Loaded Salmon Baked Potato Dinner

A low-effort single-serving dinner built around a loaded potato, canned salmon, Greek-yogurt mustard sauce, greens, and carrots.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium russet potato
- 1 5 oz can salmon, drained
- 150 g plain 2% Greek yogurt
- 100 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- 150 g baby carrots
- Mustard, to taste
- White vinegar, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Iodized salt, to taste
- Optional: sauerkraut on the side
- Optional: hot sauce or red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Microwave or bake the potato until tender.
2. Drain the canned salmon.
3. Stir the Greek yogurt with mustard, white vinegar, black pepper, and iodized salt to make a sauce.
4. Warm the spinach until wilted, or serve it raw if preferred.
5. Split the potato and top it with salmon, greens, and yogurt mustard sauce.
6. Serve with baby carrots.
7. Add sauerkraut, hot sauce, or red pepper flakes if desired.

## Notes

- This is the simpler baked-potato dinner. Use the salmon potato bean greens plate when you want more beans and fiber.
- Do not add mayo by default; the yogurt mustard sauce is the high-protein substitute.
- If the potato is unusually small, add a piece of fresh fruit.
- If the potato is unusually large, keep the yogurt sauce and carrots as written and do not add extra sides.
