# 800 Calorie Potato Egg Cottage Cheese Brunch

A savory hot brunch built around potato, eggs, cottage cheese, greens, berries, and half an avocado.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium russet potato
- 2 boiled eggs
- 250 g 2% cottage cheese
- 100 g spinach or other dark leafy greens
- 1/2 medium avocado
- 150 g frozen berries
- Everything bagel seasoning, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Optional: mustard or hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Microwave or bake the potato until tender.
2. Warm the spinach until wilted, or serve it raw if preferred.
3. Split the potato and top with spinach, sliced boiled eggs, and 1/2 avocado.
4. Add everything bagel seasoning and black pepper.
5. Serve with cottage cheese and frozen berries on the side.
6. Add mustard or hot sauce if desired.

## Notes

- This is the hot savory brunch option, separate from the oat bowl and avocado toast brunches.
- Use 1/2 avocado rather than weighing avocado.
- Do not add mayo by default; the avocado already provides the fat for the meal.
- If the potato is unusually large, reduce the berries or cottage cheese slightly.
