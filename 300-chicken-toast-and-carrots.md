# 300 Calorie Chicken Toast and Carrots

A savory, high-protein snack for times when a sweet dairy bowl does not sound satisfying enough.

## Ingredients

- 1 slice whole wheat bread, about 34 g and 90 calories
- 1 5 oz can chicken, drained
- Mustard, to taste
- 150 g baby carrots
- Optional: sauerkraut on the side
- Optional: black pepper or everything bagel seasoning

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread.
2. Drain the canned chicken.
3. Mix the chicken with mustard.
4. Put the chicken on the toast, or eat it alongside the toast.
5. Serve with baby carrots.
6. Add sauerkraut on the side if desired.

## Notes

- Do not add mayo by default; it pushes the snack well above 300 calories.
- This is the better choice when hunger feels more like a real meal is needed.
- The carrots add volume and crunch without making the snack calorie-dense.
